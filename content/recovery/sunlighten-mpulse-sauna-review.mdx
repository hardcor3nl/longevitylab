## Why Full-Spectrum Infrared Changes Everything

Most infrared saunas emit only far-infrared radiation — the wavelengths that generate heat and sweating. The Sunlighten mPulse Believe adds near-infrared and mid-infrared heaters to the far-infrared base, creating a full-spectrum system that targets different biological mechanisms simultaneously.

Understanding why this matters requires understanding what each wavelength does:

**Near-infrared (700–1400nm):**
The same wavelengths used in red light therapy panels. Near-IR penetrates tissue to 3–5cm depth, where it stimulates cytochrome c oxidase in the mitochondria, increasing ATP production and triggering cellular repair mechanisms. This is photobiomodulation occurring at sauna temperatures — a dual stimulus unavailable in conventional or far-IR-only saunas.

**Mid-infrared (1400–3000nm):**
Penetrates soft tissue to approximately 2–3cm depth. Most strongly associated with pain reduction and inflammation — mid-IR activates nitric oxide release, improving circulation and reducing inflammatory mediator concentrations. RCTs show significant reduction in fibromyalgia pain and cardiovascular improvement with mid-IR exposure.

**Far-infrared (3000nm and above):**
The heat generator. Far-IR is absorbed primarily by the skin surface and subcutaneous tissue, raising core body temperature — the mechanism behind the cardiovascular and detoxification benefits of sauna use. The KIHD study data showing 50% reduction in cardiovascular mortality with 4–7 sessions per week of sauna use is based on high-temperature traditional saunas, but far-IR achieves comparable core temperature elevation at lower ambient temperatures.

The mPulse Believe delivers all three. No other consumer sauna does.
