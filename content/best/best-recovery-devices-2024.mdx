## The Recovery Device Hierarchy

Not all recovery devices are created equal. Based on published evidence, here is the hierarchy of home recovery devices for longevity impact:

**Tier 1 — Strongest evidence:**
Sauna (traditional or infrared) — 50% cardiovascular mortality reduction with 4-7x/week use (KIHD study, n=2,315, 20-year follow-up)

**Tier 2 — Strong emerging evidence:**
Cold water immersion — 200-300% norepinephrine increase, brown adipose tissue activation, 11 minutes/week minimum effective dose (Soberg 2021)

**Tier 3 — Good mechanistic evidence:**
Red light therapy — 6,000+ published studies, strongest evidence for skin, muscle recovery, and joint pain

**Tier 4 — Adjunctive:**
Massage guns — effective for DOMS reduction and pre-workout activation

## Building Your Home Recovery Protocol
