## Why Omega-3s Matter

EPA and DHA are the two marine omega-3 fatty acids with the strongest evidence base in longevity medicine. They reduce triglycerides, lower systemic inflammation (via reduced IL-6 and CRP), and support brain structure.

The VITAL trial (25,000+ participants, 5 years) found omega-3 supplementation reduced cardiovascular death by 28% in people with low fish intake.

## The Purity Problem

Consumer Lab and IFOS testing regularly find that fish oil supplements contain:

- **Oxidized lipids** — rancid fish oil that may be harmful, not helpful
- **Label inaccuracy** — many products contain significantly less EPA/DHA than claimed
- **Heavy metal contamination** — especially in cheaper, non-certified products

This is why third-party testing certification matters more for fish oil than almost any other supplement category.
